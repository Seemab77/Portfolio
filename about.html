About Me

Engineer. Builder.
Innovator.

I'm Seemab Ramzan, a graduate Electrical Engineer from the National University of Science & Technology (NUST), Islamabad (Class of 2026). My work sits at the intersection of hardware and intelligence — designing embedded systems that think, communicate, and adapt in real time.

From deploying MQTT-based IoT networks in telecom research labs to developing non-invasive glucose monitors using IR spectroscopy and machine learning, I pursue engineering problems that matter. My internship at Korea EHT in Seoul sharpened my ability to collaborate across international teams on industrial-grade electrical systems.

I'm fluent in C/C++, Python, and MATLAB, comfortable across embedded platforms, ML frameworks, and web front-ends — and I thrive when those worlds collide. Whether it's a real-time gesture recognition system at 97–99% accuracy or a RISC-V processor built in Verilog, I bring both depth and breadth to every project.
